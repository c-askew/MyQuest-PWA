The Library

Middlesbrough Campus Library offers a variety of study environments, in zones that are designated by floor. The following details the use of each floor including information on food and drink in the library.

  • alternate_emailSucceed@Tees
    Succeed @ Tees is a series of informal workshops and seminars designed and created to help you become more effective learners with your studies at Teesside University. The workshops and seminars are full of useful information, tips and practical exercises to help you develop the skills you need to get the most from your studies and your student experience. Student & Library Services are committed to enhancing your potential in your academic studies, personal life and employment. If you need any adjustments to fully access the sessions, please contact libraryhelp@tees.ac.uk in advance.
  • bookReading Lists Online
    Reading List Online (RLO) will allow you to easily manage your module reading, and ensures that academic resources are available and accessible to you. The RLO for a module can also be accessed by logging on to the relevant E-learning@Tees site. If you are unable to find a RLO for a particular module, please contact your module leader about this. Your module reading list contains details of items like books, journals, web pages or databases. These items have been selected for you by your module leaders, so when you are doing coursework the reading list is the best place to start. However, you can (and should) also read more widely - see the sections on 'Finding Books' and 'Finding Journal Articles' for more help.
  • whatshotLearning Hub
    The Learning Hub is a learning support centre provided by Student and Library Services in partnership with the University's Schools. It is an inclusive service that is offered to all Teesside University students. It provides a space both physical and virtual where you can discuss strategies and resources to help you fulfil your potential. It provides a range of approaches for learning including drop-in, one-to-one tutorials and workshops. For more information tap here.
  • live_helpLibguides
    Libguides is our A-Z of hundreds of guides published by us to help you with whatever library needs or queries you may have. If you've got a question, this is the place to start.
